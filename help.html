How to Help

It is very important that if you think you may be suffereing from a concussion or feel any of the symptoms you talk to a medical proffesional right away. Your brain is the most important part of your body and without it you cannot funtion and go about daily buisness. If you cannot get access to a doctor, make sure you take pain medication to reduce swelling, keep your brain activity but don't push yourself, do not drive, rest and eat healthy foods. If someone you know is suffering from a concussion and is trying to hide it do the right thing and get them help
